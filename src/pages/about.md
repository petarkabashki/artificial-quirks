I began my journey in software engineering over two decades ago, grounded in a Bachelor’s degree in Mathematics. Throughout my career, I have adapted to major industry shifts—from desktop applications and N-tier .NET systems to modern cloud-native TypeScript microservices and, most recently, Agentic AI and deep learning systems.

My approach combines classical software engineering discipline with modern, data-driven technologies. I believe that trustworthy AI systems require the same level of architectural rigor, testable invariants, and runtime checks as any enterprise financial system.

## Technical Journey

### Enterprise Architecture & Conversational Platforms

As a **Lead AI Solution Engineer and Architect at Lloyds Banking Group**, I spearheaded the modernization of conversational platforms toward Generative AI and LLM-based designs. This involved authoring agentic frameworks, defining risk vectors, and leading the transition from traditional microservices to AI-driven customer service solutions.

### AI Startups & RAG

As a co-founder of **Smart City BG**, I engineered RAG-based AI assistants designed to support local municipality administration. This gave me hands-on experience building local, private LLM pipelines using Ollama, LangChain, LangGraph, and vector database structures.

### Quantitative Research & Applied Mathematics

My mathematical background (B.Sc. in Mathematics) has naturally guided my work toward quantitative analysis and algorithmic trading. I build, backtest, and evaluate systematic trading strategies using deep reinforcement learning, temporal convolutional networks, and statistical modeling tools like Kalman filters and Bayesian inference.
